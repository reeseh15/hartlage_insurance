Welcome

How we got started

Tina entered the insurance industry in 1987 by selling annuities. Soon after, she obtained her Life, Health, and Property and Casualty Licenses. Tina spent two years with Equitable Life Insurance, Co. before transferring to Community State Bank as their first, local Independent Insurance Agent. During her time at the bank, she pursued her Accounting Degree; successfully graduating with honors in 2001. Following 15 rewarding years at Community State Bank, Tina opened Hartlage Insurance Agency to provide an expanded suite of services to the community. She opened Tina's Tax Service in 2005, and has successfully operated the two offices out of her location on Main St. in Union Grove.

What we offer

  • Taxes

    Tina earned her BASc in Accounting from UW Parkside in 2001. She spent five years as an apprentice to a respected, veteran accountant to gain tax preparation experience before opening Tina's Tax Service in 2005. Tina provides tax services for individuals, estates, and businesses. She also offers payroll services for qualifying businesses. Tina meets with every client by appointment while preparing individual returns to answer questions and ensure the often complicated process is clear and understood.

  • Insurance

    Tina has been a licensed and practicing insurance agent since for over 30 years. She opened Hartlage Insurance Agency in 2004 as an independent insurance agent to have more freedom in offering the best service to Southeast Wisconsin. Tina currently represents ten insurance companies in order to customize the best policies for her clients.
